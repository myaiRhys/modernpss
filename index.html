Pioneers in Printed Shadenet Branding

For almost two decades, Printed Shadenet Solutions has set the standard for high-quality, eco-friendly fence branding across South Africa. We pioneered the use of UV-stabilised, water-based inks on durable HDPE shadenet — delivering long-lasting, vibrant branding that stands up to sun, wind, and time.

Our commitment to quality, durability, and sustainability has earned the trust of leading construction companies, developers, and event organisers nationwide.

What We Do

Construction Site Branding

Turn your site fencing into a billboard. We supply and print 50m rolls of 1.8m-high shadenet, reinforced with knitted eyelets for easy installation. Each roll carries your logo and messaging in sharp, weather-resistant colour — built to last the full project lifecycle.

Event & Festival Branding

From sports events to large-scale activations, our custom printed shadenet creates a bold visual perimeter that enhances both crowd flow and brand exposure.

Real Estate & Development Branding

Make every property launch or estate development look professional and inviting. Our shadenet branding provides a polished, consistent look while advertising your project to potential buyers.

Custom Printing & Fabric Solutions

Need something unique? We can design and print custom layouts, wraps, or shade structures to fit your exact requirements — all with the same focus on longevity and environmental care.

Why Choose Printed Shadenet Solutions

  • Quality You Can See and Feel — Every print is done on premium-grade HDPE shadenet, engineered for strength and performance in South African conditions.
  • Built for Durability — UV-resistant inks and weather-tested materials ensure your branding remains vibrant and intact long after installation.
  • Eco-Friendly Printing — Our water-based inks are safe, sustainable, and designed to reduce environmental impact without compromising colour depth or clarity.
  • Trusted Industry Experience — With nearly 20 years in the industry, we’ve built a reputation for reliability, precision, and service excellence.
  • Nationwide Delivery — Based in Cape Town, delivering across South Africa — from single-roll projects to major national campaigns.

Our Process

  1. Consultation & Quote — Share your site details and artwork — we’ll prepare a tailored quote fast.
  2. Design & Approval — Our team checks artwork for optimal print quality and sends you a final proof.
  3. Production & Printing — We print, cure, and finish your shadenet with reinforced eyelets and quality checks.
  4. Delivery — Your rolls are securely packaged and delivered to your site, ready to install.
