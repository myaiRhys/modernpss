Pioneers in Printed Shadenet Branding

For almost two decades, Printed Shadenet Solutions has led South Africa’s fence-branding industry — delivering long-lasting, vibrant prints that elevate visibility and brand presence on every site. We combine precision printing, premium HDPE materials, and eco-friendly water-based inks to ensure your brand stands out while standing up to the elements.

Our mission is simple: create products that look better, last longer, and tread lighter on the environment. Every roll of printed shadenet is proof that quality and sustainability can coexist.

Construction Site Branding

Transform temporary fencing into a professional, branded showcase. We supply and print heavy-duty, UV-stabilised shadenet rolls that withstand sun, wind, and site conditions — keeping your project neat and your brand visible 24/7. Ideal for developers, contractors, and suppliers who understand that reputation starts at the perimeter.

Available in standard 1.8 m × 50 m rolls with durable knitted eyelets for easy installation.

Event & Festival Branding

Bring life and consistency to your event spaces with custom-printed shadenet. Perfect for festivals, outdoor expos, and sporting events, our mesh banners combine visual impact with practical wind permeability. Easy to install, reusable, and designed to align with your brand’s visual identity.

From small activations to national events, we make sure your name stands out beautifully — and sustainably.

Real Estate & Development Marketing

Enhance the presentation of your developments and listings with professionally printed fence branding. Our shadenet offers both privacy and promotion, giving your projects a polished appearance while communicating key messages, logos, and contact details.

Durable enough for long-term installs yet refined enough for high-end developments, it’s the subtle edge that sets your brand apart.

Durability Meets Sustainability

Our prints are made using non-toxic, water-based inks on recyclable HDPE shadenet — an approach that reduces waste and environmental impact while maintaining exceptional print quality. Every job is produced in-house with strict quality control to ensure consistent colour, clean finish, and maximum lifespan.

We believe good business is sustainable business — and that starts with smarter materials and responsible manufacturing.
