Our Services

Durable. Visible. Built for South African conditions.

At Printed Shadenet Solutions, we specialise in **large-format shadenet printing** for the construction and events industries. Our products are designed to last — combining technical ink innovation, consistent quality, and a premium finish that elevates your brand on-site.

Large-Format Shadenet Printing

We produce bold, high-impact shadecloth branding for construction projects, property developments, and events. Each panel is printed to size and precision-aligned for large outdoor applications.

Premium Ink Technology

Our ink is scientifically engineered for shadecloth — UV stable, waterproof, and flexible. This ensures every print stays vibrant and intact even after long-term outdoor exposure. The result: branding that performs as well as it looks.

Custom Branding & Colour Options

Choose from a wide range of spot colours and layouts that align with your brand. We don’t print in full photographic colour — instead, we use a refined spot-colour process that delivers strong contrast, clean lines, and long-lasting visual impact.

Premium Finishing

Every print job is completed with care and precision — ensuring crisp edges, consistent ink coverage, and a polished presentation. It’s the final touch that sets our work apart from standard fence printing.
